# AEO Visibility Audit - VSL Script

**Duration:** 3-4 minutes  
**Tone:** Direct, conversational, slightly urgent but helpful  
**Visuals:** Screen recordings of AI searches, simple text overlays, maybe your face in corner

---

## HOOK (0:00-0:15)

**[Open with screen recording of ChatGPT]**

> "Watch this. I'm going to ask ChatGPT for the best Italian restaurant in St. George, Utah."

**[Type the question, hit enter]**

> "See those three recommendations? Notice anything?"

**[Pause, then overlay text: "YOUR BUSINESS ISN'T THERE"]**

> "If you're a local business owner and you're not showing up here... you're losing customers. Every single day."

---

## PROBLEM (0:15-1:00)

**[Cut to you on camera OR continue screen recording with voiceover]**

> "Here's what's happening right now that most business owners have NO idea about..."

> "Your customers aren't Googling anymore. They're asking AI."

**[Show quick clips of different AI searches]**

- "ChatGPT, find me a reliable plumber in my area."
- "Perplexity, who's the best real estate agent in St. George?"
- "Google, recommend a web designer near me."

> "And here's the thing — if these AI engines don't know you exist, you're invisible."

> "It doesn't matter how good your website is. It doesn't matter how many Google reviews you have. If AI can't find you or doesn't trust you enough to recommend you... you don't exist to the next generation of customers."

**[Pause for effect]**

> "This is called Answer Engine Optimization — or AEO — and 89% of local businesses aren't prepared for it."

---

## AGITATE (1:00-1:45)

**[Show stats on screen]**

> "Look at these numbers:"
- "43% of searches now START with AI instead of Google."
- "67% of users say they trust AI recommendations more than traditional search results."
- "And this is only going UP."

**[Back to camera or continue with B-roll]**

> "So let me ask you a serious question..."

> "When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity for the best [your category] in [your city]... does YOUR business come up?"

**[Pause]**

> "Do you even KNOW?"

> "Most business owners don't. They're still doing the same SEO they did five years ago, wondering why their traffic is dropping."

> "Meanwhile, their competitors — the ones who figured this out early — are getting recommended by AI over and over again."

---

## SOLUTION (1:45-2:30)

**[Shift tone — now helpful and clear]**

> "That's why I created the AEO Visibility Audit."

> "Here's what you get:"

**[Show list on screen as you go through it]**

✅ **AI Visibility Score** — I test your business across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE, and other AI platforms. You get a score from 0 to 100 showing exactly how visible you are.

✅ **Live AI Search Results** — I'll show you screenshots of what AI is actually saying about your business... or if it's recommending your competitors instead.

✅ **Competitor Intelligence** — You'll see who's winning in AI search for your category and exactly why.

✅ **Gap Analysis** — I identify what's missing: structured data, citations, reviews, authority signals, content — the stuff AI needs to trust you.

✅ **Priority Fix List** — You get the top 5 things to fix, ranked by impact, so you know exactly what to do first.

✅ **30-Minute Strategy Call** — We'll walk through your audit together and I'll answer all your questions.

> "You get a professional 15-20 page report delivered in 48 hours."

> "No fluff. Just data. Just action steps."

---

## OFFER (2:30-3:15)

**[Show pricing clearly]**

> "Normally, this kind of competitive intelligence audit would cost $297 or more."

> "But right now, I'm offering it for just $97."

> "Why?"

> "Because this is a brand new service. I need case studies. I need testimonials. So I'm running the first 20 audits at this introductory price."

> "After that, it goes to $297."

> "And honestly? It's worth way more than that when you consider how many customers you're losing to competitors who ARE showing up in AI search."

**[Shift to urgency but not scammy]**

> "So if you want to know where you stand... if you want to see the gaps BEFORE your competitors figure this out... click the button below and lock in the $97 price."

---

## GUARANTEE & CLOSE (3:15-3:45)

> "And look — I know you might be skeptical. So here's my guarantee:"

> "If you don't get clear, actionable insights showing you exactly where you stand in AI search and what to fix... I'll refund every penny. No questions asked."

> "You literally can't lose."

**[Final CTA on screen: "Get My AEO Visibility Audit - $97"]**

> "Click the button. Get your audit. Find out if AI is ignoring your business or recommending you."

> "I'll see you on the other side."

**[End with logo/contact info]**

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Visuals You Need:
1. **Screen recordings:**
   - ChatGPT search for local business category
   - Perplexity answering local query
   - Google SGE results
   - Show competitor names appearing, then overlay "YOU'RE NOT HERE"

2. **Text overlays:**
   - Stats (43%, 67%, 89%)
   - Checklist of deliverables
   - Price ($97 vs $297)

3. **Optional:**
   - Your face in corner (builds trust)
   - B-roll of you working on laptop
   - Example audit report pages (blur client names)

### Tone:
- **Conversational** — like you're talking to a friend over coffee
- **Direct** — no corporate buzzwords
- **Helpful urgency** — "this matters NOW" but not scammy

### Music:
- Background music should be subtle
- Slightly urgent/modern (not elevator music)
- Don't overpower your voice

### CTA Placement:
- Button on screen at 2:30 (when you mention price)
- Button stays visible through the end
- Link in description
- Pin comment with link
